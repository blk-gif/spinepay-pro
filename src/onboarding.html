HIPAA Privacy & Security Policy — Walden Bailey Chiropractic

As a workforce member of Walden Bailey Chiropractic, I acknowledge that I have received, read, and understand the HIPAA Privacy and Security policies of this organization.

I agree to maintain the confidentiality of all patient health information (PHI) that I encounter in the course of my work, and to disclose such information only as permitted or required by law, and only to those with a legitimate need to know.

I agree to access only the minimum necessary patient information required to perform my specific job duties, and will not access records of patients for whom I have no work-related need.

I agree to report any suspected privacy or security breach, loss of protected health information, or unauthorized access to my supervisor or the Privacy Officer immediately upon discovery.

I agree to never share my login credentials — username and password — with any other person, and to log out of the system when leaving my workstation unattended.

I understand that violations of HIPAA or organizational privacy and security policies may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination of employment, and may also result in civil or criminal penalties under applicable federal and state law.

I agree to comply with all applicable federal and state privacy laws and regulations, including the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996 (HIPAA) and the HITECH Act.

I acknowledge that patient privacy is a fundamental right and that protecting it is a core responsibility of every member of our workforce.
